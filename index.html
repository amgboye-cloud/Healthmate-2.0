    Benylin

    Benylin is an effective cough relief medicine designed to ease chest congestion and soothe irritated airways. It helps loosen and clear mucus while reducing the urge to cough, making breathing more comfortable. Suitable for cold, flu, and allergy-related coughs when used as instructed.

    Digestive Health

    Omeprazole 20mg

    Omeprazole 20mg helps reduce excess stomach acid and provides relief from heartburn, acid reflux, indigestion, and gastric ulcers. It works by blocking acid production in the stomach, offering long-lasting comfort when taken regularly. Recommended for short-term treatment and should be used as directed by a healthcare professional.

    Allergy & Respiratory

    Salbutamol Inhaler

    The Salbutamol inhaler provides fast and effective relief from asthma symptoms such as wheezing, chest tightness, and shortness of breath.
